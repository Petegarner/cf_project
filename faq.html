Why does Salesforce.com structure their pricing model on a per user per month basis?

Back in 1999 when CEO Mark Benioff first came up with the idea for Salesforce.com, cloud computing for enterprise applications was a novel idea. Back then others in the market place thought their model was crazy, but now everywhere you turn there is a new cloud based solution for everything. Enterprise CRM software used to require hundreds of thousands of dollars in servers, software licenses, employee and consulting costs and take 6 months to a year to do anything. Mark's vision was for enterprise software to be as easy to use as amazon.com. The per model per month pricing structure made it easier and more affordable for companies of all sizes to implement a best in class CRM solution.
